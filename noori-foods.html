About Noori Foods

The Largest Variety
of BBQ in Town

Noori Foods is Rawalpindi's premier destination for authentic Pakistani cuisine, world-class BBQ, Fast Food, and Chinese specialties. Over 15 years we've become the gold standard for BBQ in the twin cities.

From signature Party Platters to fresh Mughlai Handis, Karahi selections, and live Sea Food — every dish is crafted with traditional recipes, premium fresh ingredients, and cooked with passion.

Two conveniently located branches in Rawalpindi with home delivery available from 11 AM to 1 AM daily.
